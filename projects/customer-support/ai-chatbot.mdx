# AI Chatbot

Intelligent chatbot for automated customer support and lead generation.

```mermaid
graph TD
    A[Customer Message] --> B[NLP Processing]
    B --> C{Intent Recognition}
    C --> D[FAQ Matching]
    C --> E[Escalation]
    D --> F[Automated Response]
    E --> G[Agent Handoff]
    F --> H[Customer Satisfied?]
    H -->|Yes| I[End Chat]
    H -->|No| G
```
